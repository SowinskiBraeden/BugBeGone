BugBeGone
A modern simple bug tracker for all your bug reports or feature requests.
Get Started
What can you do?
BugBeGone gives many abilities for you to better manage your bug reports.
Create your team
Create your team to and invite collaborators to be on your team as either members or leaders.
View active bug reports
Open a list of submitted bug reports and assign a member or more to work on the bug.
Close the bug
Once a member has completed a bug they've been assigned to, they can close the bug.
Submit a bug/feature
If a team is accepting open bug reports, go to their team page, and submit a new bug to them.
Assign a member to a bug
A team leader can assign a team member or another team leader to a submitted bug.
Register an account
Bug Catcher
Register a bug catcher account if you're just looking to submit bug reports to a team. This basic accounts can still greatly support the projects you care about.
Register Now
Collaborator
Register a collaborator account to join or create teams, close bugs you're assigned to. You can still submit bugs to your own, or other teams.
