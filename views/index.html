Have Any Questions?

Here are some questions you may be thinking of

What is BugBeGone?

BugBeGone is an all in one bug tracker for your projects or businesses.

It allows anybody to report a bug to your team, then you can assign a member to solve the bug.

How can I start?

To start off, simply register an account. If you are here to report a bug, you can search your desired project with its team code.

If you are here to run a bug tracker, create or join a team. If you create a team, invite members, and get to work!
